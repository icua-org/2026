About the Conference

The 2026 International Conference on Urban Analytics brings together leading researchers, practitioners, and policymakers from around the world to explore cutting-edge developments in urban data science, spatial analytics, and smart city technologies.

As cities face unprecedented challenges from climate change, rapid urbanization, and technological transformation, the need for sophisticated analytical approaches to understand and manage urban systems has never been greater. This conference serves as a premier platform for exchanging ideas, presenting innovative research, and fostering international collaborations in urban analytics.
